Connection

Preview in Simulation now, or connect Live to your MQTT broker when the hardware is ready.

Mosquitto needs a WebSocket listener — in mosquitto.conf:
listener 9001
protocol websockets
then restart. Use wss:// for TLS.
The reader auto-detects fields (co2, soil, temperature, state…) and reads TTN's decoded_payload, so defaults work for a standard setup.
Simulation streams realistic readings so you can verify the scene, chart, topology, log and pump toggle — no broker needed. Watering visibly raises soil moisture and wakes the plant.
